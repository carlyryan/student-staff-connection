About

Mission

The mission of the Student-Staff Research Connection Application is to manage
the matching of interests of students and lecturers when applying for postgraduate study opportunities.

Functionality

The system allows students to create a profile, search for staff that match their department or interest, view staff profiles,
and request contact with potential staff collaborators. Staff are also able to create profiles, search for and view student profiles,
receive notifications about interested students, and manage requests properly.
